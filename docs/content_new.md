### Apron integration

Capture and pay bills seamless with the new Apron and FreeAgent integration.

Read more

FreeAgent and Apron are building a two-way synchronisation and payment integration. This partnership will allow bill, contact and payment data to flow seamlessly between Apron and FreeAgent. 

By connecting these platforms, users can sync their payables data instantly. They can initiate single or bulk payments directly from the FreeAgent interface, with all activity automatically reconciled back to an Apron bank feed. Alternatively, they can remain in Apron, capture and pay bills, and have the data synced to the rest of their bookkeeping in FreeAgent.

### Rental statement extraction for landlords

Easily transform messy rental statements into neat, digital entries. We’ll automatically extract income and fees from agency statements, match them to your properties and assign accounting categories.

Read more

Reconciling a single bank payment against a complex list of rent, management fees and repair bills is a major headache. We’re using automation to transform those messy PDF statements into neat, digital entries that align perfectly with your bank transactions.

By automating the categorisation and reconciliation process for landlords, we’re removing the disjointed manual workflow that leads to errors, ensuring your property accounts are always accurate and up to date.
